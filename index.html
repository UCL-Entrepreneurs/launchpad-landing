About Moonshot Launchpad Get to know us

Moonshot Launchpad is UCL's first student-led innovation programme for tech/engineering/science startups to develop breakthrough, sustainable business models. Starting in November 2016, Moonshot Launchpad is running a 12-week business plan competition featuring 3 streams: Energy, Healthcare, and Fintech (with Deutsche Bank Labs). Up to 36 startups will receive academic/corporate mentorship, essential workshops with industry-leading speakers (held at UCL, IdeaLondon, Wayra, Deutsche Bank Labs and other venues across London) and gain insight into the startup ecosystem in London and beyond. The winners of each stream will receive dedicated services and advisory from the best in the field, and an opportunity to pitch to a panel of London's top investors as part of the UCLe Fund.

Phase Details



Challenges

Two simultaneous 12-week-long business plan competitions, focusing on tackling present and future challenges in the sectors of Energy and Healthcare, with support from industry leaders in the public and private sector. Participants will obtain non-financial support in the form of Idea Surgeries, and access to coworking spaces and R&D facilities, in order to elevate their projects to a point of no return. The challenges will culminate in a final pitch to a panel of expert judges, maintaining an essential balance of technology and business strategy. Finalists will compete for funding in the UCLe Fund, receive 1:1 mentorship from specialists and will have the opportunity to create partnerships with industry leaders in the form of:

  • R&D Support
  • Proof-of-concept testing
  • Distribution
  • Market Insights
  • Commercial Partnerships




Idea Surgeries

High-intensity workshops, dedicated to addressing all the the needs of project teams. The Idea Surgeries will be structured according the stages of development of the Challenge, based on mandatory surveys taken 2 days prior to each session. Support will be provided by Moonshot Launchpad staff, and energy/healthcare specialists from corporate and academic backgrounds, on a pro bono basis.

Year-long, Moonshot Launchpad will be developing Dojo, an open online platform for insight and support on the A-Z of starting a company in university. Dojo will feature an online student-generated practical curriculum and a reactive community of innovators, where teams can find the most efficient milestones towards survival and, ultimately, growth. Topics covered will include, but not be limited to, Idea Generation & Validation, Marketing, Proof-of-concept, Team-building, and Entrepreneurial Finance.

Moonshot Launchpad will also be running various off-cycle 2-day Challenges, in partnership with market leaders, driving innovation in industries not served directly by the main program, such as Fintech, Built Environment and Cybersecurity.
