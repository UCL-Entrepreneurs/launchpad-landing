Moonshot Launchpad LiftOff

About Moonshot Launchpad Get to know us

UCL Entrepreneurs Society is launching Moonshot Launchpad, a student run innovation programme for engineering, science and tech startups. It encompasses a comprehensive 12-week pre-accelerator for startups in Energy, Healthcare and Fintech, filled with opportunities to learn new skills, build a network, and make your idea a reality.

At the end of the programme, students will pitch to a sector-focused panel of judges, receiving exposure to industry leaders and a chance to win dedicated services from our partners, an opportunity to pitch at the UCLe VC Fund and much more.

Programme Details

You will benefit from a series of “Idea Surgeries”, workshops where you can learn the essentials of building, validating and marketing your idea, hosted at fantastic locations such as IdeaLondon and Wayra. You will also receive mentorship from business advisors and technical gurus to an unparalleled degree of intimacy.

Prizes

At the end of the programme, you will pitch to a sector-focused panel of judges, receiving exposure to industry leaders and a chance to win the following:

  • Winners receive dedicate services from companies such as MedCity, KPMG Tech Growth, Global App Testing, Rapid Product Development Ltd., MedTech Engine, and more
  • Advisory from industry leaders and experts in the field, such as Ashley George (Global Innovation Director of GSK).
  • An opportunity to pitch in the UCLe Fund, where you will get exposure to top investors and have the chance to receive grant funding!

Timeline

  • Now: Applications open.
  • November 17th: Kick-off Event (for participants).
  • November-February: Programme (Idea Surgeries, Insight Events, Café Talks and more…)
  • Week of February 6th: Final Judging Round
  • Week of February 20th: UCLe Fund
