About Moonshot Launchpad Get to know us

UCL Entrepreneurs Moonshot Launchpad is a multi-phase pre-accelerator run by students, for students. Our vision is to create a self-sustainable ecosystem of innovation in technology, where breakthrough projects are grown into stable businesses and trained to thrive in the real-world.

Moonshot Launchpad has two distinct phases.
Phase 1 - will run in Term 1, September - December, and feature two business-plan challenges in the sectors of Health and Energy.
Phase 2 - will run in Term 2, January - February, where the Accelerator will take place.

Throughout both phases, Moonshot Launchpad will be developing Dojo, an open platform for insight and support on the A to Z of starting a company in university. Dojo will feature an online student-generated practical curriculum and a reactive community of alumni innovators, where teams can find the most efficient milestones towards sustainable growth. Topics covered will include, but not be limited to, Idea Generation & Validation, Marketing, Proof-of-concept, Team-building, and Entrepreneurial Finance.
